What date do you want to claim Employment Support Allowance from?

You cannot claim NS ESA for any dates you were paid SSP.

Your claim should start the day after your SSP ends. For example, if your SSP ends on 25 October 2022, you can claim NS ESA from 26 October 2022.

You will need evidence such as a fit note (also known as a sick note) to cover the period you want to claim for.
