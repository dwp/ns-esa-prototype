What date do you want to claim from?

You can claim from:

  • the day after your Statutory Sick Pay ends - this date is on form SSP1, if you have one
  • a date up to 3 months ago if you have fit notes (you might call these sick notes) to cover the period

This date will be confirmed with you to make sure you're getting the money you're entitled to.

In exceptional circumstances, we can consider backdating beyond 3 months if you can provide evidence that you intended to claim sooner but were unable to do so. If so, put and tell us later by phoning the number on your decision letter.
