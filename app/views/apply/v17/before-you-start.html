Before you start

You will not be able to save your answers and come back to the form later. But you’ll get a chance to check and update your answers before you send it.

Make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society account details
  • doctor's surgery name and address

You’ll also need details of the following, if they apply to you:

  • current or recent work
  • the date any Statutory Sick Pay ended or will end

If you do not have a bank account, you can use the account of a trusted family member or friend. Find out how to get a bank account if you want to set one up.
