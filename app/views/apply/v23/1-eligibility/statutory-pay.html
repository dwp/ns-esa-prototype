Are you getting Statutory Sick Pay?

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) is paid by your employer in the same way as your wages. 

If you’re not sure, check our information on SSP and eligibility (opens in a new tab).

You can apply for New Style ESA if you do not get Statutory Sick Pay or it ends in the next 3 months.
