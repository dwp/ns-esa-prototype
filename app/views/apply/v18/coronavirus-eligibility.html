Important

On 24 March 2022, the temporary regulations that provided eligibility to Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) if you had been affected by coronavirus will expire.

Find out how it will affect your claim.

Is coronavirus (COVID-19) the main reason for your claim?

Select yes if one of the following applied to you or your child before 24 March 2022:

  • you were self-isolating because you had symptoms of coronavirus in line with government regulations
  • you were self-isolating because you got a notification from NHS or public health authorities that you had come into contact with someone with coronavirus (COVID-19)
  • you were advised by your doctor or healthcare professional to self-isolate before going into hospital for surgery
