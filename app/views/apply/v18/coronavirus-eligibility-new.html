Important

You only apply for ESA because of coronavirus (COVID-19), before 24 February 2022.

Is coronavirus (COVID-19) the main reason for your claim?

Select yes if one of the following applied to you or your child before 24 February 2022:

  • you’re self-isolating because you have symptoms of coronavirus
  • you’re self-isolating because you came into contact with someone who has symptoms of coronavirus. Or you were told to self-isolate by a test and trace service
  • you’ve been advised by your doctor or healthcare professional to self-isolate before going into hospital for surgery
  • you’re quarantining because you have returned from another country (opens in new tab)
