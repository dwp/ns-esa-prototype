Pension or annuity Are you getting regular payments from a pension or annuity?

You do not need to tell us about:

  • money that you pay into a pension or annuity
  • money that you pay into a pension or annuity
I need more information

You might be getting money from a pension or annuity if you:

  • retired from your job because of ill health
  • inherited a pension or annuity from someone who died
  • reached normal minimum pension age (55 years old) and asked your pension or annuity provider to start paying you

If you are getting money from a pension or annuity you need to tell us how much you get and where it comes from. We will use this information to work out the amount of New Style ESA you might be entitled to.
