Apply for New Style Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

Before you apply make sure you have your:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank or building society details
  • GP surgery name and address

You'll also need details of any:

  • current or recent work
  • pensions scheme's you're paid from
  • permanent health insurance or income protection payments you're getting

After you've applied

You might also need to make an appointment or provide more information before you can get Employment and Support Allowance.

You'll be told what else you need to do at the end of this application.
