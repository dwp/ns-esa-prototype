Apply for New Style Employment and Support Allowance (New Style ESA)

Use this service if you need financial support because you are off work with a health condition, injury or disability.

Check if you are eligible

You might be eligible if you:

  • have paid or been credited with National Insurance in the last 2 or 3 tax years
  • do not get Statutory Sick Pay, or it is due to end in the next 3 months
  • are under State Pension age

If you are not sure how much National Insurance you have paid, you can check your National Insurance record online. You will need to set up or sign in to an online account to check.

What to expect

The online form is the first stage of your application.

You will be asked questions about:

  • your health
  • any paid work
  • any voluntary work
  • any statutory payments

You do not need to send any medical evidence now. The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) will contact you if they need you to get it in the future.
