Apply for New Style Employment and Support Allowance (New Style ESA)

Use this service if you need financial support because you are off work with a health condition, injury or disability.

What to expect

The online form is divided into 2 parts.

First, you'll be asked some questions to find out if you're eligible for New Style ESA. Depending on your answers, there might be a different benefit that's more appropriate for you.

If New Style ESA is the right benefit for you, you'll go into the main application form. There you'll be asked questions about:

  • your health
  • any paid work
  • any voluntary work
  • any statutory payments

You do not need to send any medical evidence now. The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) will contact you if they need you to get it in the future.
