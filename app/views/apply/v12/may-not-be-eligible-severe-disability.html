Based on your answers, you may not be eligible for New Style ESA

You cannot claim New Style ESA if you get or you’re entitled to the severe disability premium.

What to do next

Call Jobcentre Plus to check if you’re entitled to the severe disability premium.

Jobcentre Plus

Telephone: 0800 169 0350 (choose option 1)

If you’re not entitled to the severe disability premium, then you can apply for New Style ESA.

If you’re entitled to the severe disability premium, you may be able to claim contribution-based or income-related Employment and Support Allowance instead.
