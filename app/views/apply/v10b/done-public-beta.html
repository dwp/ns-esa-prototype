You’ve completed the first part of your application

Things to do now

1. Make an appointment

Call 0800 328 5644 and make an appointment to:

  • prove your identity
  • agree what you’ll do in return for getting Employment and Support Allowance

Lines are open Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm and closed on public holidays.

2. Prepare evidence to support your application

You’ll need to bring the following if you have them:

  • proof of identity
  • fit notes (you might know these as ‘sick notes’)
  • an SSP1 form from your employer, confirming when your Statutory Sick Pay ends
  • P45 form
  • wage slips for the last 2 months
  • most recent pension statement
  • most recent permanent health insurance statement
