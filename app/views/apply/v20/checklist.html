Before you start

On the following pages we’ll ask you for information to help us decide if you’re eligible for new style Employment Support Allowance.

Below is a list of what we’ll ask you for. It will save time if you have these ready before you start your application.

It might seem like a lot but it’s things you probably already know. We’ll just be asking for details such as dates and amounts.

  • Your email address, phone number and National Insurance number
  • Your doctor’s name, phone number and practice address
  • If you’re currently staying in hospital, the name of the hospital and the date you went in
  • What illnesses, conditions and disabilities you have, including the date they started
  • Details of any work you’re doing, paid or voluntary, including your earnings and hours you work
  • Whether you’ve been paid Statutory Sick Pay (if your employer has given you a form SSP1, it will help to have it in front of you)
  • Any other statutory payments you’ve had, such as maternity or paternity pay
  • Whether you’ve received payments from your employer’s permanent health insurance
  • Details of a bank or building society account that we can pay any benefit your entitled into
