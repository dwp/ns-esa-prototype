Apply for ‘new style’ Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

You're eligible to apply if you:

  • need money because you're off work with an illness, injury or disability
  • have paid National Insurance in the last 2 or 3 tax years
  • are not getting Statutory Sick Pay, or it's due to end in the next 3 months
  • are under State Pension age

If you're not sure whether you've paid National Insurance, you can check your National Insurance record online. You'll need to set up or sign in to an online account to check.

What to expect

The online form is the first stage of your application.

You'll be asked questions about:

  • your health
  • any paid work
  • any voluntary work
  • any statutory payments
  • any income you get from a pension or income protection insurance
Once you've completed the online form, you'll need to make an appointment to prove your identity and agree what you'll need to do in return for getting 'new style' Employment and Support Allowance.

You might also need to go to a Work Capability Assessment to see how your disability or health condition affects your ability to work.
