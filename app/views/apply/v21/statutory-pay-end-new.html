What is your Statutory Sick Pay end date?

This might be different from the last date you were paid SSP.

It can be a date in the past or up to 3 months from today.

You can find this date on your SSP1 form. If you do not have an SSP1 form, you need to get this information from your employer.

For example, 13 07 2022
