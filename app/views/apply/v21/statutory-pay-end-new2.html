What is your Statutory Sick Pay end date?

This might be different from the last date you were paid SSP. You can find this date on your SSP1 form.

How to find this date:

  • If you have an SSP1 form, check the very last question “Have you been paying your employee SSP?” The end date should be given here.
  • If you do not have an SSP1 form, you can get this date from your employer.
It can be a date in the past or up to 3 months from today. For example, 13 07 2022
