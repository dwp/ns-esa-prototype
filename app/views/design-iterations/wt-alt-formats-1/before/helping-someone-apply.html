Helping someone apply for New Style ESA

You can help someone apply for New Style ESA if:

  • the applicant is giving you the answers
  • the applicant can be contacted about the claim

Complete the application as if you are the applicant. Do not enter your own details.

If you’re an appointee, deputy, power of attorney or a corporate acting body, use the telephone application.
