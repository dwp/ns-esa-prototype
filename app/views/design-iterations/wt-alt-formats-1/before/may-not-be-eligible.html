Based on your answers, you may not be eligible for New Style ESA

New Style ESA supports you if you have a disability or health condition that affects your ability to work. If you’re on a low income or out of work, you can apply for Universal Credit (opens in new tab) or New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA) (opens in new tab) instead.

Or you can continue to apply for New Style ESA if you still think you’re eligible for it.
