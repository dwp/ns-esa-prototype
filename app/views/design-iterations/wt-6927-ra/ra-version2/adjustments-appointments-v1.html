Adjustments for appointments in person

To assess your claim for New Style ESA we may ask you to attend an appointment at a DWP building. If you need any adjustments for this, you can tell us about them.

For example, you might need:

  • a ramp, lift or ground floor appointment
  • a wide doorway
  • someone with you for your appointment

These adjustments will not affect the outcome of your claim.
